About Us

Mens Post Collegiate Club Lacrosse team based in Brooklyn, NY. Member of the American Lacrosse League. Founded on Aug. 2005 - Parade Grounds - Prospect Park, Brooklyn, NY.

We are comprised of former (and current) Division 1, Division 2, Division 3, MCLA, MLL, and International lacrosse players.

Our home field is Aviator Sports Complex in Brooklyn.

Our Masters team competes in the Southern CT league in Springs and Sound Shore during the Summer.
